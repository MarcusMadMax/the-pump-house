About

“Our mission here at the Confidence Project: to develop skills and provide opportunities for motivated students to excel on national and world stages.” In the short time that PForm.nz have been running their program, their performers have starred in an off-Broadway musical on 42nd Street in New York City, danced onstage at Disneyland, taken out 30+ placings at competitions in NZ and Australia, and headlined the NZ premiere of Trolls World Tour… not to mention the countless other performances we’ve tucked under our belt along the way. It’s been a WILD ride. PForm.nz’s second annual Confidence Project showcase was always set to be special. Now, after everything 2020 threw at us, it’s the ultimate celebration of tenacity and unrelenting positivity. Join them as they honour the love that these young people have for each other, and as they herald in a new era of success and passion. This little family conquered 2020, now they’re out to conquer the world.
